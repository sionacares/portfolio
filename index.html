SIONA GROUP is a social enterprise that applies technology to facilitate social change and reduce social vices. Relying on the goodwill of individuals and government agencies who organise free skill based programs or absorb apprentices, we set up a platform that serves as an information hub for skill acquisition opportunities for disadvantaged youths (aged 16-25) who may ordinarily not have access to such information. The platform also serves as a means for such individuals to connect with prospective trainers and register for programs which would build or enhance their skills in areas of their choice.Read more

Who we are

SIONA GROUP is a social enterprise that has been set up to provide a viable, technologically-driven solution to the problem of enemployment in our society with a view to reducing crime and other social ills associated with the problem. Our main focus are the uneducated/minimally educated and disadvantaged youths who may ordinariy constitute a menace to the society;we believe that if these individuals are actively engaged and their energies are properly harnessed,they would contribute positively to their various communities and the nation at large.

What we do

We have established an online platform that connects interested youths to free and optimal vocational training, enterpreneuiral and apprenticeship programs that are run by individuals, Government agencies and Non-Governmetal Organisations



Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide up-to-date information about available vocational trainings and apprenticeship programs to the uneducated youth so they can empower themselves.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be leading solution center to vocational skills-based employment in Nigeria.
